There are no triumphs, only the arches remain

A pedestrian bridge leading nowhere, or rather from a residential district to a cemetery is awkward on its own. The once planned generous housing development stopped prematurely leaving the bridge over the deserted road meaningless. Truly, “there are no triumphs, only the arches remain”.

 

Arc of Triumph, a project by The Východné pobrežie (Košice, SK)

The continent called Europe lies on the edge of the contemporary world of innovations. The land called Slovakia can be found on the edge of the continent that lies on the edge of the contemporary world of innovations. The town called Košice lies on the edge of the land on the edge of the continent that lies on the edge of the contemporary world of innovations. The Sídlisko Ťahanovce suburban project lies on the edge of the town on the edge of the land on the edge of the continent that lies on the edge of the contemporary world of innovations. Finally, on the edge of the suburb situated on the edge of the town that lies on the edge of the land on the edge of the continent on the edge of the contemporary world of innovations, a huge concrete pedestrian bridge stretches over the road.

Košice is not a part of any conquering superpower that celebrates its supremacy, growth and triumphs by building monuments. Nevertheless, Košice can still become an important creative conqueror whose journey to eternal glory employs wit and wisdom instead of dangerous weapons. The architecture of the upper bridge reminds one of an Arc of triumph, either the two most famous ones in Paris or the smaller arches in Barcelona, London and other European cities. Obviously, these countries built their arches of triumph as a reward for exhausting clashes or victory over difficult-to-conquer areas. What is it that gives Košice the right to reward its citizens with an Arc of triumph? The opportunity to build Triumph memorials without the presence of any actual Triumph? One can even build an Arc of Triumph to celebrate the Victory over Triumphs. In this post-modern age, one can say that There is no Triumph, only Arcs that remain.
