Tomáš Džadoň
Arts

a precious building with a human face

Tomáš Džadoň uses the local refferences from his childhood in most of his visual art pieces. These are often the archetypes of the eastern block prefab buildings that meanwhile became a folklore. Not in all of his works he tackles the architectural topics too.

However, when he replaces a Portal of a historical building with a gate from an archetypal prefab block of flats known from every village and town in Slovakia and Czech Republic, he actualizes an architectural theme. It is also his specific way of humanizing something precious and untouchable.

 

Portal, a project by Tomáš DŽADOŇ (Poprad, SK / Prague, CZ)

I installed a typical prefabricated house entrance from an apartment building where I grew up, on a Gothic house in the center of Brno. Because socialism radically transformed our environment, I decided to retroactively simulate “slight change” according to the motto of Prague Spring “Socialism with a human face.” (Tomáš Džadoň, 2009)
